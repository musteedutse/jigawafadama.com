The Jigawa state government is among the benefiting states of Federal Government grant to support citizens affected by COVID-19 pandemic. The Nigerian COVID-19 Action Recovery and Economic Stimulus NG-CARES programs the coordinating body of CARES programs across the focal states. Hence the Jigawa COVID-19 Action Recovery and Economic Stimulus (J-CARES) program came into force. The Fadama structure is coordinating the food security and livelihood components of the programme under result area II, the Programme Development Objective (PDO) is to protect the livelihood and food security of poor and vulnerable families and facilitate the recovery of local economic activity in the state.

Selection, Sensitisation and mobilisation of FCA

The first process of j-cares program is selection, sensitization and mobilisation of beneficiaries, a standing team were formed by His Excellency the Governor of Jigawa State Malam Umar A. Namadi FCA. for the smooth and fair implementation of this loudable program .

CARP Preparation

COVID-19 Action Recovery Plan known as (CARP) will be prepared after the successful selection of deserving beneficiaries by the standing committee by the Fadama Facilitator under the guidance of Fadama Office .

Desk/Field Appraisal

The Local Government Desk Office will be the one to judge the Carp after its prepared for assesment, estimation and valuation.

Technical Review

A Fadama Technical Team will review the CARP for final approval.

Approval of CARP

After the CARP is been review by the technical team form by Fadama Coordinator, then the approval will be given which is a green light to engage service providers .

Engagement of Service Providers

An invitation will be sent to service providers for procurement procedures .

Delivery of Inputs & Services

NG-CARES Programme is an intervention that is built on the platforms of existing projects of the Federal Government of Nigeria. These include National Cash Transfer Programme (NCTP), Youth Employment and Social Support Operations (YESSO), Community and Social Development Project (CSDP), FADAMA, Government Enterprise and Empowerment Programme, (GEEP). These projects are operational at the Federal and State level.
